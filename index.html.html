BIO

My name is Ashlynn Hodges. I am 24 years old, and I am an Entertainment Technologies major at BRCC with my sequence being in film. I enjoy movies, music, and the outdoors. I hope to one day work in the Art Department for a production company working with set design and props. I would also enjoy working for music festivals and different live venues. I am very interested in interactive art installations that are incorporated in music festivals and sometimes in different cities that allow public art installations. I enjoy making the world a creative place and I am a big believer in not letting artists be creative for us. I think that everyone has the ability to imagine and create, and I hope to inspire that in others.
